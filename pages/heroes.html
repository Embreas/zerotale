Heroes

Heroes are bruisers who can use two different weapons to dish out physical damage at close to medium ranges, with a few long range skills available.
They're a very forgiving class due to their built-in tankiness that cannot be obtained through passive and active skills.
This combination of passives and actives allow heroes to escape difficult situations trough dashes and crowd control.
They're jack of all trades, containing a little of everything, making the hero class an excellent choice for beginners and veterans alike.

Archmages

Archmage is the latest advancement in the Wizard class tree.
It specializes in high DPS to a large number of monsters as well as sufficient crows control abilities.
You are the mid to long-range DPS in the party.
this class has a rather heavy dependency upon ping to the server as some of the most crucial skills requiere good ping to truly shine.

Saviors

Savior is a defensive class who engages in close-ranged combat, they have the highest stamina in the game and the capability to absorb and whithstand a lot of damage.
Saviors are the shield of the party, and excellent team players, their various buffs, crowd control, and endurance gives them the ability to protect their allies and boost their damage.
They can feel a bit lacking in the damage department compared to other classes however, they can become stron just like anyone else.
it's a class suited for beginners to veterans alike, so if you enjoy a class with lots of crowd control, endurance and survivability, then savior may be for you.

Windstalkers

Windstalkers is your jack of all the trades adventurer. With three different weapon option (dagger and shield, bow or crossbow), you have the option to customize to your preferred playstyle with options for close range, long range, single target specialists or mob extermination.
The biggest perk for windstalker is the item drop rate and ely drop rate. No other class will ever be able to compare their ability to maximize loot.
